What We Do & Why We Do

Feedie Food Rescue is a cutting-edge waste food donation and management app dedicated to reducing food waste and fighting food insecurity.

Our mission is to connect food donors with organizations in need, ensuring that surplus food reaches those who can benefit from it the most.

Join us in the fight against food waste and food insecurity. Together, we can make a significant impact on our communities, reduce environmental strain, and ensure that no one goes hungry.
